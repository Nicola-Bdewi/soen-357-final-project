FAQ

Find the answers for the most frequently asked questions below

How do you ensure the safety of your food for people with allergies?

Absolutely! We take allergies very seriously. Our platform provides clear labeling and descriptions for all food and drink options, allowing consumers to make informed decisions about what they order.

What measures are taken to prevent cross-contamination?

Yes, we have strict measures in place! We ensure minimal risk of cross-contamination by following rigorous food safety protocols and maintaining separate preparation areas for different types of food.

How do you cater to customers with specific dietary needs?

We offer a solution for individuals to explore and order food tailored to their unique dietary requirements with confidence. Our platform allows customers to customize their orders according to their dietary needs.

What allergens are your meals free from?

Our application is targeted toward those suffering from allergies to dairy, gluten, soy, or eggs as well as those on a vegan diet. However, we cater to a wide range of dietary needs and restrictions.

How do you ensure the cleanliness of your food?

We prioritize cleanliness! We follow strict food safety and hygiene protocols to ensure the cleanliness of our food. This includes regular cleaning and sanitization of our preparation areas.

Can customers specify their preferences to avoid allergens?

Of course! Our platform allows customers to clearly record their preferences, ensuring minimal risk of cross-contamination or accidental mix-ups.
